# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, background, experience level, nationality, or identity.

---

## Our Standards

**Behaviour that helps this community thrive:**
- Being kind and respectful in all interactions
- Welcoming newcomers and beginners
- Giving constructive, helpful feedback
- Accepting different opinions and approaches gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Behaviour that is not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive comments
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the project
- Sharing others' private information without permission

---

## Our Responsibilities

The project maintainer is responsible for clarifying acceptable behaviour and will take fair and appropriate action in response to any behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct.

The maintainer has the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, and contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainer directly via GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community spaces tied to this project.
